ABOUT THE EVENT

The Department of Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is proud to organize Cyber Shield 2K25, an online cybersecurity contest at K.S.Rangasamy College of Technology. This event brings together students from across the country to participate in exciting cybersecurity challenges. With the theme "Think Before You Click!", we aim to promote cybersecurity awareness and safe online practices through fun and engaging competitions.

OUR MISSION

At Cyber Shield 2K25, our mission is to raise awareness about cybersecurity and promote safe online practices. We aim to educate students about the importance of cybersecurity through engaging contests and challenges, fostering a culture of digital safety and responsibility.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where every student is aware of cybersecurity threats and practices safe online behavior. Our goal is to inspire the next generation to "Think Before You Click" and become responsible digital citizens who can protect themselves and others in the digital world.
